Frequently Asked Questions

How do I understand my blood test results?

Select your lab panel, enter the values from your report, and LabAssist will instantly tell you which values are normal, low, or high — with a plain-language clinical summary.

What panels are supported?

Complete Blood Count (CBC), Liver Function Tests (LFT), Renal Function Tests (RFT), Thyroid Function Tests (TFT), Arterial Blood Gas (ABG), Lipid Profile, Diabetes Panel, Iron Studies, Vitamins, and Hepatitis Markers.

Is my data stored or shared?

No. LabAssist does not store, log, or share any values you enter. Everything stays in your browser session only.
